About MSU Science Olympiad

We are a college student organization dedicated to supporting Science Olympiad across Michigan. Our volunteers help run tournaments, develop educational resources, and create event kit designs all to empower middle and high school students to excel in STEM competitions.

What We Do

Tournament Volunteering

Our members volunteer at regional and invitational tournaments across Michigan, serving as event supervisors, test writers, and coaches.

State Tournament

Each year, MSU hosts the Michigan Science Olympiad state tournament. Our members help to create this world-class competition experience.

Resources & Kits

We develop comprehensive resources, study guides, and build event kit designs to help teams prepare and tournaments run smoothly.
